Application Guidelines

Before You Begin

  • Review program requirements and eligibility criteria
  • Prepare all required documents in digital format
  • Have your academic history information ready
  • Prepare contact information for references

Required Documents

  • Official transcripts from previous institutions
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement
  • Copy of government-issued ID
  • Passport-sized photograph

Application Timeline

  • Fall Semester: Application deadline - June 30
  • Spring Semester: Application deadline - November 30
  • Applications are reviewed within 4-6 weeks
  • Admission decisions are sent via email
